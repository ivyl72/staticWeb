  • Lithops are cool
    • Lithops are small, interesting plants from southern Africa that are relatively easy to grow indoors.Lithops (the word is both singular and plural, and both the scientific name and a common name) are found in arid regions in widely separated, sparsely populated colonies. The first Lithops was discovered by William John Burchell in 1811 during a botanical expedition in southern Africa. During his travels in the Northern Cape Province, he found a strange looking brown stone with a fissure across the surface near the town of Prieska.
